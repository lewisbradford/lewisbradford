About me

Your comfort zone is a beautiful place but nothing gets done there, does it?

I have been working in digital design for two years, I’m self taught with a huge desire for making things pretty and simple, it’s pretty simple really.

I’m currently helping change the face of orthodontics with DB Orthodontics working in the in-house design and marketing team.

My passion for digital design stems from my interests focused on technology, human behaviour and feeling compelled to design creative strategies for users.
